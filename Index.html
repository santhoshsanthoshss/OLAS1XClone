When can I purchase the all new S1 X and S1 X+ ?

The S1 X range of scooters are available in three different variants - S1 X+, S1 X (2kWh), and S1 X (3kWh). The S1 X+ is available to purchase now at a price of ₹1,09,999. The S1 X (3kWh), and S1 X (2kWh) scooters are available for reservation for ₹999 at a price of ₹99,999, and ₹89,999 respectively.

I have reserved the S1 X (2kWh or 3kWh), when can I buy it?

The S1 X (3kWh), and S1 X (2kWh) scooters are available for reservation for ₹999. The purchase window will soon open in September 2023 and the exact date for the purchase window shall be communciated via email.

I have reserved the S1 X (2kWh or 3kWh), what will be the price
that I need to pay during purchase?

The reservation amount of ₹999 will be adjusted from your scooter price during the final purchase.
